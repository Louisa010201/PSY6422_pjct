Data collected in the Global Burden of Disease Study 2019 (GBD 2019) Contraceptive Prevalence Estimates 1970-2019. This data came with an existing codebook in "data" then "Codebook.csv"

Instructions to load and save data:

To load the data for this project follow the doi below:
Global Burden of Disease Collaborative Network. Global Burden of Disease Study 2019 (GBD 2019). Contraceptive Prevalence Estimates 1970-2019. Seattle, United States of America: Institute for Health Metrics and Evaluation (IHME), 2022. https://doi.org/10.6069/WZHR-V063	

Once on the webpage, select files, then download the file "Contraceptive Prevalence Estimates(CSV)". After this has downloaded, open the file "IHME_GBD_2019_CONTRACEPTIVE_PREVALENCE_1990_2019_DATA_ALL_WOMEN_Y2022M07D23.CSV". Save this file in "data" and name it "data.csv". 

This data set shows contraceptive Prevalence estimates from the years 1990-2019.															

In the data frame "cf1" all the world regions dataframes have been joined into one column named "region". These include: 
- central europe, eastern europe and central asia
- high income
- latin america and caribbean
- north africa and middle east
- south asia
- southeast asia, east asia and oceania
- subsaharan africa
